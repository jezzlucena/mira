Description of Service

Mira is a habit-tracking and mood-logging application for iOS and watchOS. The app allows you to track habits, record mood on a 1–6 scale, view analytics and correlations, and optionally sync data across devices via iCloud.

The app is provided in two tiers:

  • Free tier: Includes all core features — unlimited habits, mood logging, full analytics, data export/import, HealthKit integration, and ad-free experience.
  • Premium tier ($1.99/month or $17.99/year): Adds iCloud Sync across devices and Apple Watch companion app.
